Who I Am

My name is David Wulbrecht, or Dave for short. I am 31 years old and am currently going to the University of Michigan for a Master's Degree in UX/UI Design and Research

I graduated high school in 2009. From there, I went to GVSU for 3 years before joining the Marine Corps in 2013 for 5 years. In the Marines, I worked as a communications technician to fix radios, computers, etc. After I got out, I went to Oakland University for 3 years for a degree in IT. While there, a served as vice president for the Student Veterans of Oakland University and now, at UofM, I serve as vice president for the UofM chapter.
